The TuneEditor application is not difficult to use.
  1. Enter all of your tune-related fields on in the boxes on the left.
  2. As you edit, the file will be updated in the window on the right.
  3. Click 'Save' when you are happy with it, and the file will be saved.
  4. To create a new file, click 'New'. This doesn't create much - basically it strips away most information, leaving only the directory, transcriber name, and the note length.
  5. Please be respectful of the work of others - do not attribute other's work to yourself. Not cool.
